Welcome, volunteers!

You will find the results of your Improbable Beef belly button bacteria sample below. Simply select the random ID number you were assigned and the page will do the rest! You will see a bar chart showing the top ten bacterial species found in your belly button. As well as a bubble chart showing all of the bacteria we were able to culture from your sample and their respective amounts. You can hover over both the bar and bubble charts to see the names of the bactiera. We also provided a gauge diagram that shows your self-reported weekly washing frequency. Once Improbable Beef identifies a species of bacteria as a candidate to manufacture their synthetic beef, you will be able to determine if your sample contains that bacteria.
